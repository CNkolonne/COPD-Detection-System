Educational Focus

What is COPD?

Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease is a chronic inflammatory lung disease that causes obstructed airflow from the lungs.

air

Wheezing

A whistling sound made while breathing, often a primary indicator of narrowing airways.

personal_injury

Shortness of Breath

Difficulty catching breath, especially during physical activities or as the condition progresses.

sick

Chronic Cough

A persistent cough that may produce mucus (sputum) that's clear, white, yellow or greenish.

How to Prevent COPD

While some risk factors are genetic, most cases are preventable through lifestyle choices and environmental awareness.

Smoking Cessation

The single most effective way to reduce the risk of COPD or stop its progression.

  • check_circle Avoid secondhand smoke
  • check_circle Use nicotine therapy if needed

Air Quality

Limiting exposure to pollutants, dust, and fumes in your living and working environment.

  • check_circle Improve home ventilation
  • check_circle Wear masks in dusty areas
vaccines

Vaccinations

Reducing respiratory infections that can trigger or worsen lung damage.

  • check_circle Annual flu shots
  • check_circle Pneumococcal vaccine
